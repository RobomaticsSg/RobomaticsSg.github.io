About Robomatics Pte Ltd

At Robomatics, we are dedicated to providing comprehensive solutions to meet the varied needs of our clients. We have expanded our services since our establishment in 1999 to offer One-Stop-Solutions that cater to your manufacturing, process, and production requirements. Our expertise extends to various areas, including machine building, where we specialize in designing and building machines that are tailored to meet the specific needs of our clients. We also specialize in robot integration, which involves incorporating robots into your production line to improve efficiency and accuracy. In addition to machine building and robot integration, we also offer the supply of automation parts such as PLCs, control components, pneumatic components, and hydraulic components. We understand the importance of safety in any production environment, and as such, we provide safety solutions that are tailored to meet your specific safety requirements. We also provide weighing solutions that are designed to ensure accuracy and efficiency in your production process. Our coding and printing solutions allow you to easily and accurately label your products. We also provide code readers that allow you to easily scan and identify products, as well as laser engraving services that can be used for branding, labelling, and marking. At Robomatics, we understand the importance of traceability in production, and we provide traceability solutions that allow you to easily track your products throughout the production process. Finally, we also provide IoT solutions that allow you to connect your machines and devices to the internet, enabling you to collect and analyse data in real-time, leading to more informed decision-making and improved efficiency.
